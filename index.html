Other/Creative Work
“byrd_bot”—a dynamic web application, which sources an archive of bird songs to sonically
represent differences in anthropogenic species displacement in New Jersey between 1970
and 2017 and 2065. The project is being developed in collaboration with Dr. Charles Schutte
(Environmental Science, Rowan University).
“feels_bot”—an interactive exhibit constructed from JavaScript that utilizes artificial intelligence and
crowdsourced .gifs to “catch feels” from users (e.g., to identify and respond to emotions detected
from users). Physically installed as part of the “Pull Request” art exhibition in the Vachon Gallery at
Seattle University Sept.-Oct. 2018.
22
“sylla_bot”—a Python-based chatbot that uses machine learning to learn, and answer questions about, a
course syllabus.
